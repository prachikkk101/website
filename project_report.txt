1. Executive Summary
The Gas Pipeline Project Management System (GP-PMS) is a purpose-built internal web application for Oxygen Protech Pvt. Ltd. It directly replaces the Microsoft Excel master sheet currently used to track all operations at city-gas distribution (CGD) pipeline sites in Punjab. Every sheet, every column, every tracked metric from that workbook becomes a live database module in this application.
The reference design is the HCG (Haryana City Gas) portal at admin.hcgonline.co.in — your screenshots show exactly the kind of professional, data-dense tool this needs to be. The visual style, navigation pattern, KPI dashboard layout, data tables with green headers, and modal-based data entry forms are all directly adopted from that reference.

12. Glossary
Term
Definition
CGD
City Gas Distribution — piped natural gas infrastructure replacing LPG cylinders
PNG
Piped Natural Gas — gas delivered to premises via pipeline
BP Number
Business Partner Number — customer's unique ID in the gas authority (AG&P/HCG) billing system
PE Pipe
Polyethylene pipe — underground gas distribution main (sizes D20, D32, D63, D90, D110, D125mm)
GI Pipe
Galvanised Iron pipe — indoor service piping within customer premises
LMC
Last Mile Connectivity — final connection from distribution main to customer's premises
I&C
Installation & Commissioning — installing meters/regulators and activating supply at commercial sites
RA Bill
Running Account Bill — interim payment bill submitted to gas authority for work done to date
GA
Gas Authority — distribution company (AG&P, HCG) that awards the contract
TF
Tapping Fitting — branch fitting from main PE pipe to service line
HDD
Horizontal Directional Drilling — trenchless technique for laying pipe under roads
SCMH
Standard Cubic Meters per Hour — gas flow rate for meters and regulators
RFC
Readiness For Commissioning — stage where GI work is complete, ready for meter
JMR & Gas-In
Joint Meter Reading & Gas Inauguration — final step, gas supply activated at customer premises
LHS / RHS
Left Hand Side / Right Hand Side — meter mounting position (from HCG meter installation form)
DC No.
Delivery Challan Number — reference on goods delivery note for tool transfers
HCG Portal
Haryana City Gas portal (admin.hcgonline.co.in) — reference UI for GP-PMS design
JWT
JSON Web Token — stateless authentication standard used for API security
